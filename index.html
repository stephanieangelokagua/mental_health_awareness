Mental Health

Mental health is an essential part of our overall well-being. It encompasses our emotional, psychological, and social health, influencing how we think, feel, and behave each day. Mental health affects how we handle stress, relate to others, make choices, and navigate challenges in life. Just as physical health enables us to move and function, mental health empowers us to cope with life's ups and downs, build relationships, and achieve our potential. Good mental health doesn't mean always being happy or free from problems; rather, it's about having the tools and resilience to manage difficult moments and recover from setbacks. On this website, we aim to provide an understanding of common signs of mental health issues, ways to manage stress, and valuable resources for support. Whether you're exploring mental health for yourself or others, our goal is to create a supportive space for learning and growth.

Signs

Drastic Changes in Eating or Sleeping Patterns

Noticeable changes in eating habits, such as eating significantly more or less, or sleeping patterns, like insomnia or oversleeping.

Changes in Mood or Behaviour
